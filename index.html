“Christchurch is lucky to have a gem like this. Not only are the creations delicious, but everything is personally made with care.”

We produce authentic French viennoiserie, sourdoughs, ciabattas and baguettes. Our bread is made with organic or spray-free flour, all sourced within the Canterbury region. We use traditional techniques and long fermentation to bring you the most delicious baked goods in town.
